Remembering India's Independence Day

India gained independence from British rule on August 15, 1947. This day is celebrated with great enthusiasm and patriotic fervor.

This national holiday marks the end of a long struggle for freedom led by inspiring leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, and many others.
